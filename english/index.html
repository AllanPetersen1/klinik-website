Dental Treatments

Close to central Copenhagen you find the dental practice of Dentist Rikke Mørch where we provide a full range of dental treatments. The dental practice is ideally located close to main roads and with parking spaces nearby. It is also very easy to find our practice using public transportation.

The practice is equipped with modern dental facilities to provide you with optimal dental care. We aim at giving our patients the best possible treatment in a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere, thereby making your visit as comfortable as possible.

Our team provides a full range of dental treatments, e.g. examination for cavities, gingivitis, and periodontal problems, crowns and bridges, implants, veneers, tooth whitening, cosmetic treatment, and advanced restorative procedures. Our dental surgeon provides surgery when needed.

We cooperate with recognized dental technicians in Denmark as well as abroad for crowns, bridges, implants, etc., and we always use the best materials for these treatments. We are very much looking forward to welcoming you at our dental practice. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

The Dental Clinic
